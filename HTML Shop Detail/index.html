The Namiki Emperor is a Classic Piece of Japan’s heritage. It is handmade by Japan’s greatest artisans. The pen is covered in Urushi Lacquer which required the artisans to spend hours coating the pen with a small brush. Applying the lacquer can take up to 2 years and if they artisan’s make a single mistake they start over. The pen is the product of hard work and is part of a Japanese tradition that has been going on for years and years. Some might feel that it is just a pen, but no the pen is a work of art and labor. It might look muted, but speaks volumes about the labor and the love that is put into making the pen.
